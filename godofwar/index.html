THE STORY

Kratos is a father again. As mentor and protector to Atreus, a son determined to earn his respect, he is forced to deal with and control the rage that has long defined him while out in a very dangerous world with his son.

From the marble and columns of ornate Olympus to the rugged forests, mountains, and caves of pre-Viking Norse lore, the violent and chaotic journey of the Ghost of Sparta continues in God of War.

For the first time in the series, there are no loading screens. The cinematic camera never cuts, creating a seamless and immersive experience.

CHARACTERS

Kratos

The Ghost of Sparta has left his bloody past in Greece behind and now lives as a man in the realm of Midgard. He must put aside the rage that has driven him for many years as he teaches his son to survive.

Atreus

Kratos' son, a curious and intelligent boy who just wants to understand what it means to be a god. His desire to prove himself worthy of family and responsibility becomes increasingly important.

Mimir

The self-proclaimed Smartest Man Alive and former advisor to Odin. This severed head of Norse mythology serves as the duo's guide, providing knowledge about the Nine Realms.

Baldur

The Norse god of light and the estranged son of Odin and Freya. Baldur's invulnerability to all threats physical or magical has left him unable to feel anything, including pain or joy.

Freya

A powerful witch living in the Wildwoods, Freya is the former queen of the Valkyries and mother to Baldur. Her deep connection to nature and magic makes her both ally and adversary.

GAMEPLAY FEATURES

LEVIATHAN AXE

The mighty Leviathan Axe is a powerful and versatile weapon. Throw it to freeze enemies, solve puzzles, or recall it with satisfying precision to your hand.

BLADES OF CHAOS

Kratos' classic weapons return with new combat abilities. The fiery chains and brutal attacks offer crowd control against numerous enemies.

ATREUS COMPANION

Atreus serves as a capable companion in battle, following your commands to shoot arrows while learning to fight alongside his father.

VALKYRIE BATTLES

Optional but incredibly challenging encounters against the most powerful enemies in the game, requiring mastery of all combat mechanics.

NINE REALMS

Explore the interconnected realms of Norse mythology, each with unique environments, enemies, stories, and rewards to discover.

CHARACTER PROGRESSION

Customize Kratos build with armor, talismans, and skills, while Atreus gains new abilities that open up new combat options.
